Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide an estimate?

Most estimates are provided within 24 hours of your initial contact. For urgent requests, we often provide same-day estimates during business hours.

Do you charge for estimates?

No, all estimates are completely free with no obligation. We believe you should have all the information you need to make an informed decision.

How far in advance should I schedule service?

While we can often accommodate same-week requests, we recommend scheduling 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during peak cleaning season (spring and fall).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of services unless other arrangements have been made in advance.
