Roof Cleaning FAQs

Is roof cleaning safe for my shingles?

Yes, our soft washing technique uses low pressure (under 500 PSI) combined with specialized cleaning solutions that kill organic growth without damaging shingles. We never use high-pressure power washing that can strip away protective granules.

How often should I clean my roof?

In the Pacific Northwest, we recommend roof cleaning every 2-3 years. Homes with heavy tree coverage or north-facing roofs may need annual maintenance to prevent moss buildup.

Do you offer moss prevention treatments?

Yes, we apply zinc-based preventative treatments after cleaning that inhibit moss and algae growth for 12-24 months. This is highly recommended to extend time between cleanings.

Will cleaning remove dark stains?

Our process removes 90-95% of dark algae stains (Gloeocapsa magma). Some older, deeply embedded stains may lighten but not completely disappear. We'll provide a realistic expectation after inspection.
