About Our Services

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Redmond and the surrounding Greater Seattle Area. Our central location allows us to efficiently serve customers throughout the eastside communities while maintaining prompt, reliable service.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Eastside Softwash is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance. We provide proof of insurance upon request and ensure all work is performed to local code requirements.

What makes your cleaning methods different?

We use a combination of soft washing and pressure washing techniques, choosing the appropriate method for each surface. Our eco-friendly cleaning solutions are biodegradable and safe, while still being highly effective at removing organic growth and stains.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance programs?

Yes, we offer customized maintenance programs for residential and commercial properties. Regular maintenance helps prevent major buildup and extends the life of your property's exterior surfaces.
