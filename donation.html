Supporting Our Community

At Eastside Softwash, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported our business. Our donation program helps fund local community initiatives, environmental conservation efforts, and support for families in need.

Environmental Conservation

Community Programs

Funding local community centers, youth programs, and initiatives that strengthen our Greater Seattle Area neighborhoods.

Emergency Relief

Providing assistance to families and individuals facing unexpected hardships or emergency situations in our local community.

Donation FAQs

How are donations used?

All donations go directly to support local community initiatives including emergency assistance, environmental conservation, and community programs. We provide regular updates on how funds are distributed.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Please consult with your tax advisor regarding the deductibility of donations. We can provide documentation of your contribution for your records.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Yes, if you have a specific preference for how your donation should be used (environmental, emergency relief, etc.), please contact us and we'll do our best to honor your request.

How will I know my donation made a difference?

We send quarterly updates to all donors showing the impact of contributions, including stories from community members who have been helped and environmental projects supported.
