What is Commercial Soft Washing?

Soft washing is a low-pressure cleaning method that utilizes specialized, biodegradable cleaning solutions to safely remove organic stains (like mold, mildew, algae, and bacteria), dirt, and grime from delicate exterior surfaces. Unlike traditional pressure washing, soft washing uses minimal pressure, similar to a garden hose, ensuring no damage to your building's more sensitive materials.

Ideal for Surfaces Such As:

  • Stucco & EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish Systems)
  • Painted Wood Siding & Trim
  • Vinyl Siding & Aluminum Siding
  • Roof Shingles (Asphalt, Composite)
  • Delicate Stone & Brick Veneers
  • Outdoor Fabric Awnings & Canopies
