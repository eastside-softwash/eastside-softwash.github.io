Frequently Asked Questions About Commercial Softwashing

   
     
What is the difference between soft washing and pressure washing?
     
       

Soft washing uses low pressure combined with specialized cleaning solutions to safely remove organic growth (algae, mold, mildew) and dirt from delicate surfaces like stucco, EIFS, painted wood, and siding. It's ideal for surfaces that could be damaged by high pressure. Pressure washing uses high-pressure water streams to tackle tougher surfaces like concrete, brick, and pavers, effectively removing stubborn stains and grime.

     
   
   
     
How often should a commercial property be soft washed?
     
       

The frequency depends on several factors, including your property's location, exposure to elements, surrounding vegetation, and business type. Generally, we recommend a professional soft wash every 12-24 months to maintain optimal appearance and prevent long-term damage. We can create a customized maintenance plan for your business.

     
   
   
     
Are your cleaning solutions safe for landscaping and the environment?
     
       

Yes! We prioritize the safety of your landscaping, employees, customers, and the environment. Our cleaning solutions are professional-grade, biodegradable, and designed to be safe when applied correctly. We take precautions to protect surrounding plants and areas.

Absolutely! Many commercial clients opt for our recurring maintenance plans, which ensure your property consistently looks its best without you having to remember to schedule services. We can tailor a plan to fit your specific needs and budget.
