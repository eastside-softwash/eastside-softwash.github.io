Frequently Asked Questions About Commercial Exterior Cleaning

What distinguishes soft washing from pressure washing?

Soft washing employs low-pressure water combined with specialized, eco-friendly cleaning solutions. This method is ideal for gently and effectively removing organic growth (like algae, mold, and mildew) and dirt from delicate surfaces such as stucco, EIFS, painted wood, and siding, preventing damage often associated with high pressure. Pressure washing utilizes high-pressure water streams and is reserved for more durable surfaces like concrete, brick, and pavers to remove stubborn grime, oil stains, and graffiti.

What is the recommended frequency for commercial exterior cleaning?

The optimal cleaning schedule for your commercial property depends on various factors, including its geographical location, exposure to environmental elements, proximity to vegetation, and the nature of your business operations. Typically, we advise professional exterior cleaning every 12-24 months to maintain optimal appearance and prevent long-term surface degradation. We are happy to develop a tailored maintenance plan specific to your business needs.

Are your cleaning solutions safe for surrounding landscapes and the environment?

Yes, absolutely. We prioritize the health of your landscaping, the safety of your employees and customers, and environmental responsibility. Our professional-grade cleaning solutions are biodegradable and formulated to be safe when applied according to industry best practices. We implement thorough protective measures for all surrounding plants and vulnerable areas during our cleaning processes.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance agreements for businesses?

Indeed! Many of our commercial clients opt for our convenient recurring maintenance contracts. These plans ensure your property consistently maintains a pristine appearance without the need for manual scheduling. We can customize a maintenance plan to align perfectly with your specific requirements and budgetary considerations.

What is your typical response time for quotes and service initiation?
