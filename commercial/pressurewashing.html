Why Commercial Pressure Washing?

Pressure washing is a highly effective method for cleaning durable exterior surfaces that accumulate tough dirt, oil, grease, graffiti, and other stubborn contaminants. Using high-pressure water, our professional-grade equipment can quickly restore the cleanliness and safety of your commercial property's hardscapes.

Ideal for Surfaces Such As:

  • Concrete Sidewalks & Walkways
  • Parking Lots & Drive-thrus
  • Dumpster Pads & Loading Docks
  • Brick Walls & Masonry
  • Stone Patios & Hardscapes
  • Heavy Equipment & Machinery (exterior)

The Eastside Pressure Washing Advantage:

  • **Deep Cleaning Power:** Removes even the most embedded dirt, oil, and grime that other methods can't touch.
  • **Improved Safety:** Eliminates slippery algae and mildew from walkways, reducing trip hazards for clients and employees.
  • **Enhanced Aesthetics:** Drastically improves the visual appeal of your property, leaving a fresh, professional look.
  • **Health & Hygiene:** Removes harmful bacteria and pollutants, contributing to a healthier environment around your business.
  • **Efficient & Fast:** Our powerful equipment and experienced team ensure a quick and effective cleaning process, minimizing disruption.

Whether it's restoring discolored concrete or removing graffiti, Eastside Softwash has the expertise and equipment to handle all your commercial pressure washing needs.
